census_2022_rosenheim {sora}R Documentation

Census 2022 dataset for Rosenheim, Germany

Format

A dataframe with 189 rows and 5 columns: crs = 3035

id

CellCode from INSPIRE GeoGitter Germany (500 meter grid)

pop_sum

Sum of population of census 2022.

pop_diff_sum

Total difference of population between census 2011 and 2022.

x

x coordinate of centroid of grid cell

y

y coordinate of centroid of grid cell

Source

The dataset is based on Census data of Germany. The grid data is from BKG.
